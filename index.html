Illumination

Illumination at IIT Kharagpur is a cherished tradition during Diwali. Each student hall creates intricate patterns using earthen lamps (Diyas) on large bamboo structures, showcasing themes and designs. Students create elaborate light displays and installations across the institute, illuminating the campus with a vibrant, festive atmosphere. Various student clubs and organizations compete to design the most creative and captivating light-based art exhibits.Know more about Illu

Spring Fest

Spring Fest, known as SF (motto: True Spirit of Youth), is the annual social and cultural festival of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur. It’s held during January in the spring semester, and the first edition took place in 1960. Over the years, Spring Fest has grown in scale, attracting participants from all over India and abroad.Know more about Spring Fest

Kshitij is the annual techno-management symposium of IIT Kharagpur. It’s a vibrant event that celebrates the spirit of science and technology, bringing together students from across India to showcase their scientific and managerial prowess1. Started in 2004, Kshitij has grown to become Asia’s largest Techno-management symposium. The fest includes corporate simulations, robotics, olympiads, hackathons, and more. Kshitij
